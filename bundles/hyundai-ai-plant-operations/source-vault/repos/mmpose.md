# mmpose

OpenMMLab pose estimation — HRNet, ViTPose, and other architectures for human and object keypoint detection.

**GitHub:** https://github.com/open-mmlab/mmpose
**Stars:** 7,622

## Use Cases
[[UC-03 Component Validation]] · [[UC-04 SOP Compliance]]

## How It's Used
For UC-03: detects mounting point keypoints on seats and brackets to verify alignment. For UC-04: extracts human skeleton (17-point COCO format) to feed into action recognition for SOP compliance monitoring.

## Technologies
HRNet, ViTPose, top-down pose, bottom-up pose
